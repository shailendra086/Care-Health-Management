About of Sunil Mishra

Sunil Mishra is a young and dynamic Orthopaedic doctor having professional experience of more than 10 years. He has joined SCI Hospital as Senior Consultant Orthopaedic Surgeon. He has completed his basic medical education (MBBS) from the prestigious Maulana Azad medical College, New Delhi & later pursued his post graduate training in Orthopaedic surgery (MS, Orthopaedics) from Safdarjung Hospital.

He did his Senior Residency in Orthopaedic Surgery from Lok Nayak Hospital (Maulana Azad medical College), Delhi & was awarded the Diplomate of National Board by National Academy of Medical Sciences.
